About Afrocentric Visions

Celebrating African creativity and cultural excellence through curated digital art.

Our Story

Afrocentric Visions was founded with a singular vision: to create a platform that celebrates and amplifies authentic African artistic expression in the digital age. Starting as a passion project by a collective of artists, curators, and cultural enthusiasts, we've grown into a thriving community dedicated to bringing exceptional Afrocentric art to homes, offices, and creative spaces worldwide. Our journey began with a recognition that African and diaspora artists deserved greater visibility and accessibility, and we've committed ourselves to making that vision a reality through carefully curated collections and direct artist partnerships.

Our Mission

We are committed to transforming how the world experiences and values Afrocentric art. Our mission is to create meaningful connections between talented artists and art enthusiasts, while celebrating the richness of African heritage, culture, and contemporary creativity. We believe in the power of art to inspire, educate, and unite communities. Every piece in our collection is thoughtfully selected to represent authentic cultural expression, artistic excellence, and the diverse voices of Africa and its diaspora. We stand for inclusivity, authenticity, and the transformative power of creative vision.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our art collection and services.

Our digital prints are available in multiple formats including high-resolution JPG, PNG, and PDF files. Each format is optimized for different uses - JPG for web and social media, PNG for graphics with transparency, and PDF for professional printing. All files are provided at 4K resolution (3840 x 2160 pixels) or higher, ensuring exceptional print quality up to 36x24 inches. When you purchase a digital print, you'll receive download links for all available formats, giving you complete flexibility for your project needs.

We offer fast worldwide shipping with an average delivery time of 5 business days. Orders are carefully packaged to ensure your framed artwork arrives in perfect condition. Once your order is placed, you'll receive a tracking number within 24 hours so you can monitor your shipment. For expedited delivery options, please contact our customer service team at 1stdigitalartistry@gmail.com. We also offer free shipping on orders over $200 to most countries. All framed pieces are insured during transit for complete peace of mind.

We stand behind every product with our 100% Satisfaction Guarantee. If you're not completely satisfied with your purchase for any reason, we offer a full refund within 30 days of purchase. For digital prints, simply let us know within 30 days and we'll process your refund immediately. For framed artwork, we accept returns within 30 days if the piece arrives damaged or doesn't meet your expectations - we'll arrange return shipping and provide a full refund or replacement at no cost. Your satisfaction is our top priority, and we want you to love your Afrocentric art!
